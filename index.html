    Message from villenoir

    Passionate about the beautiful wine-growing landscapes, we specialize in producing exceptional wines that cater to current consumption trends.

    The vineyards

    Our vineyards are strategically located in a region known for its stunning wine-growing landscapes.

    We cultivate around 20 hectares of vines which benefit from favorable climatic conditions, protected from extreme conditions. The majority of our plots of vines are devoted to the production of red grapes. The grape varieties planted are Merlot, Cabernet Sauvignon and Petit Verdot. A small area of the vineyard is also planted with white grape varieties; Sauvignon Blanc and Semillon.

    Soil

    Our soils are lean and shallow, well-draining with hard bedrock below.

    Climate

    Billowing morning fog gives way to warm afternoon sunlight. Come evening, a salty breeze rustles through the vines.
